## 🎨 Image Style Guidelines

### **Color Palette to Match:**
- **Primary**: Blue (#03499e)
- **Accent**: Cyan (#00b4d8)
- **Secondary**: Light blue (#0066cc)
- **Neutral**: White, light gray backgrounds

### **Image Characteristics:**
✅ **DO:**
- High resolution (minimum 1920x1080 for hero)
- Professional and modern aesthetic
- Natural lighting
- Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- Blue/cyan color tones when possible
- Diverse and inclusive representation
- Technology-forward imagery

❌ **AVOID:**
- Overly staged or cheesy stock photos
- Dark or gloomy imagery
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Low resolution images
- Outdated technology
- Overly formal or stiff poses

---

## 📐 Recommended Image Sizes

### **Hero Section:**
- Desktop: 1920x1080px or larger
- Mobile: 1080x1920px (portrait)
- Format: JPG or WebP

### **Feature Cards:**
- 800x600px
- Format: JPG, PNG, or SVG

- 400x400px (square)
- Format: JPG or PNG

### **About/Team Photos:**
- 1200x800px
- Format: JPG or WebP

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Consistency**: Choose images from the same photographer or collection for visual consistency
2. **Licensing**: Always check usage rights and attribution requirements
3. **Optimization**: Compress images before uploading (use TinyPNG or similar)
4. **Alt Text**: Prepare descriptive alt text for accessibility
5. **Responsive**: Consider how images will look on mobile devices
6. **Brand Alignment**: Ensure images reflect your brand values and professionalism
